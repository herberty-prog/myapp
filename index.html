空腹対策
Morning hunger - HARDEST
05:00 - 11:00
The first hurdle of 2-meal transition. Hunger peaks last about 15min. The first 2 weeks are the challenge.
  • Drink 500ml-1L water (fills stomach physically) 0kcal
  • Black coffee 1-2 cups (suppresses appetite) 0kcal
  • Lick a pinch of salt (mineral supplement, prevents dizziness)
  • Xylitol gum (chewing suppresses hunger hormones) 5kcal
After lunch - afternoon
13:00 - 17:00
  • 15 almonds (keep at desk) ~90kcal
  • Sugar-free konjac jelly ~5kcal
  • Drink water and wait 10 min (often confuse hunger and thirst)
Commute home - DANGER ZONE
18:00 - 20:30
  • Protein bar before leaving work (under 15g carbs) ~150kcal
  • Keep nut pack in glovebox ~60kcal
  • Dizziness or palpitations = low blood sugar - eat immediately (do NOT endure)
Before bed
22:00 - 23:00
  • Hot unsweetened herbal tea 0kcal
  • Brush teeth (appetite naturally disappears)
  • NEVER: snacks, instant noodles, extra rice
Mindset
  • Hunger peaks last only 15 min - drink water and ride it out
  • Hunger = fat burning - recognize it as a positive sign
  • Body adapts in 2 weeks - hormones adjust
  • Dizziness/palpitations are different - low blood sugar, eat immediately
